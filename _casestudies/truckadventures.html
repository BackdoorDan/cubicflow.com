Cubicflow Case Study

The only event where kids can experience all the trucks they love — in the driver’s seat.

The Challenge

Truck Adventures wanted to up their game big for their 2nd annual event. They knew that growing exposure, building a brand, and really solidifying their web presence would all be key to increasing ticket sales. Our combined efforts led to an increase in over 400% in ticket sales with soaring conversion rates.

Brand Identity

How Cubicflow Helped

Truck Adventures didn't really have a brand their first year, so for their 2nd annual event we really wanted to make sure they made the right impression. We created a corporate identity and logomark to launch alongside their completely new website complete with an e-commerce and ticketing system. We then used targeted social media campaigns to funnel people to the website.

Rich Welch, President, Truck Adventures
